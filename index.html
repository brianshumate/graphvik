GraphVik is my nerdy visual tribute to world renown artist & awesome human

It is a small visualization experiment that consists of 1500 tiles which were programmatically derived from a photo of Mr. Muniz. These tiles are arranged in a force directed graph in which all connected nodes randomly radiate from a central node.

“GraphVik” is also a play on the word graphic. ;)
